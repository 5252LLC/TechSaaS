Toast Placement Options
Placement Customize
Toasts can be placed in any corner or center of the viewport.
Stacking Multiple
Multiple toasts will automatically stack vertically.

For positioning toasts in your application, use the following CSS classes: position-fixed top-0 end-0 p-3 (top-right), position-fixed bottom-0 end-0 p-3 (bottom-right), position-fixed top-0 start-0 p-3 (top-left), or position-fixed bottom-0 start-0 p-3 (bottom-left).
